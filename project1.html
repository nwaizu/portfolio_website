Attendance Management System

The Attendance Management System is a web application designed to streamline staff attendance and shift management. It allows administrators to record attendance, monitor punctuality, and generate reports efficiently.

Problem Solved

Many organizations face challenges tracking employee attendance manually. This system reduces errors, saves time, and improves overall management efficiency.

Features

  • Record and manage staff attendance
  • Generate monthly and weekly reports
  • Track lateness and absences
  • User-friendly interface with authentication

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL
